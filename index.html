Hosein Hasanbeig



Short Bio:

I am currently a Ph.D. Candidate in the Department of Computer Science at University of Oxford under the supervision of Daniel Kroening and Alessandro Abate. Before, I was a research assistant in the Systems Control Lab at University of Toronto where I received my M.Sc. in 2016. I obtained my B.Sc. from University of Tehran.


My research focus is on the design and analysis of safe machine learning algorithms by employing notions from formal methods. Most of my work is at the intersection of reinforcement learning, logic, and game theory.
